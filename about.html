History of R&S Trading

The owners of R&S Trading, Mr. Sherwin Madera and Ms. Rose Ann Madera, began as mere employees. Mr. Sherwin Madera began his career as a Platinum Karaoke promoter and put a lot of effort into saving money. However, he felt that his salary as an employee was insufficient, so he made the decision to invest and market the items on his own. Initially, he started selling at home until he saved enough money to set up his own business establishment.

Ms. Rose Ann began her career as a smartphone promodiser, just like her business partner. She once worked as an employee that promotes an Oppo smartphone brand. She sought to save money so that she could open her own business since, like her brother Mr. Sherwin, she is aware that her salary as an employee is insufficient.

They both made the decision to co-own and operate their own business. They established a business called R&S Trading in Labo, Camarines Norte, and expanded it until they opened a new branch in Daet, Camarines Norte.
